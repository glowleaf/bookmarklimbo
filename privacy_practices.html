Privacy Practices for Bookmark Limbo Chrome Extension

This document outlines the data handling practices of the Bookmark Limbo Chrome extension. Our extension helps you manage your bookmarks more effectively with AI-powered features while respecting your privacy.

Data Collection and Usage

When you use Bookmark Limbo, the following data may be collected and processed:

  • Bookmark Data:
    • The extension accesses your Chrome bookmarks to provide its functionality
    • Bookmark URLs, titles, and folder structure are processed locally within your browser
    • No bookmark data is stored on external servers without your explicit consent
  • AI Processing:
    • When you use AI features (like summarization or categorization), the following occurs:
      • The bookmark title and URL are sent to our AI service
      • The AI service processes this information to generate summaries and categories
      • Results are returned to your browser and stored locally
      • We use OpenAI's API for AI processing, subject to their privacy policy
  • Local Storage:
    • Extension settings and preferences are stored locally in your browser
    • Generated summaries and categories are cached locally for better performance
    • You can clear this data at any time through the extension settings
  • Social Features:
    • If you choose to share bookmarks:
      • The shared bookmark data (URL, title, your comments) will be posted to the selected platform
      • Sharing is always opt-in and requires your explicit action
      • Platform-specific privacy policies apply when sharing (Twitter, LinkedIn, etc.)

Required Permissions

Bookmark Limbo requires the following Chrome permissions:

  • bookmarks - To access and manage your Chrome bookmarks
  • storage - To save your preferences and cached data locally
  • tabs - To interact with your current browser tabs when adding bookmarks
  • identity - For social media integration (optional)

Third-Party Services

  • OpenAI API:
    • Used for AI-powered features like summarization and categorization
    • Only bookmark titles and URLs are sent to OpenAI
    • Subject to OpenAI's privacy policy and data handling practices
  • Social Media Platforms:
    • Integration with Twitter, LinkedIn, etc., is optional
    • Authentication and data sharing follow each platform's privacy policies
    • You can revoke access to these platforms at any time

Data Security

  • All communication with our servers uses secure HTTPS connections
  • API keys and authentication tokens are stored securely in your browser
  • No personal data is stored on our servers without explicit consent

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your locally stored data through the extension settings
  • Delete your local data at any time
  • Opt-out of AI features and social sharing
  • Uninstall the extension, which will remove all local data

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated as we add new features or modify existing ones. Significant changes will be announced through the extension's update notes.
