Bookmark Limbo Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2024

1. Introduction

Bookmark Limbo ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Bookmark Data

We collect and store:

  • Bookmark URLs
  • Bookmark titles
  • Bookmark creation dates
  • Whether you've visited bookmarks

2.2 Settings Data

We store your extension settings, including:

  • Limbo duration preferences
  • Display preferences
  • Integration settings (Telegram, social media, etc.)

2.3 API Keys and Tokens

If you choose to use integrations, we store:

  • OpenAI API key (for summaries)
  • Social media access tokens (if configured)
  • Telegram integration tokens (if configured)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Display your bookmarks in the new tab page
  • Generate AI summaries of bookmarked content
  • Track which bookmarks you've visited
  • Maintain your preferences and settings
  • Enable social media and Telegram integrations

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. We do not:

  • Send your bookmarks to our servers
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Collect personal information beyond what's necessary for the extension to function

5. Third-Party Services

When using integrations, data may be processed by:

  • OpenAI (for generating summaries)
  • Social media platforms (when using their APIs)
  • Telegram (when using Telegram integration)

These services have their own privacy policies and data handling practices.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data
  • Delete your stored data
  • Disable integrations
  • Uninstall the extension

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store.
