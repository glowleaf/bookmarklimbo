Bookmark Limbo

A Chrome extension that helps you manage your bookmarks with AI-powered summaries

Smart Bookmark Selection

Shows three bookmarks at a time (old, medium-aged, and new) to help you systematically work through your backlog.

AI-Powered Summaries

Get quick summaries of your bookmarked content to help you decide what to keep and what to let go.

Social Media Integration

Works with Twitter, Facebook, Reddit, and Telegram to help you manage content from all your favorite platforms.

Clean Interface

Simple, distraction-free design that helps you focus on managing your bookmarks.

Install from Chrome Web Store
